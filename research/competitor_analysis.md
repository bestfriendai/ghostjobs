# Competitor Analysis: GhostJobs

## Direct Competitors

### 1. Indeed (indeed.com)
**Strengths**: 
- Largest job board, massive reach
- Established trust
- Resume upload

**Weaknesses**: 
- Source of ghost jobs, doesn't solve problem
- No ghost detection
- Cluttered UI

### 2. LinkedIn Jobs
**Strengths**:
- Professional network
- Company insights
- Easy apply

**Weaknesses**:
- Has zombie listings
- No scam detection
- Premium required for features

### 3. Glassdoor
**Strengths**:
- Company reviews
- Salary data

**Weaknesses**:
- Not focused on job verification
- Limited job search features

## Indirect Competitors

### 4. CareerCloud
**What they do**: Job search tools and resources

**Differentiation**: GhostJobs focuses specifically on ghost detection

### 5. Rippling (Ghost jobs articles)
**What they do**: HR platform that writes about ghost jobs

**Differentiation**: Not a product, just content

## Competitive Advantages

1. **First mover** in automated ghost job detection
2. **AI-powered** analysis (not just manual research)
3. **Simple UX** - just paste URL, get score
4. **Mobile-first** - job searching happens on phones
5. **Browser extension** for seamless scanning

## Threats

1. Indeed/LinkedIn could add similar features
2. Could be seen as "negative" product
3. Detection accuracy needs to be high
