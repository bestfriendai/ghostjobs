## Tweet 2/12
The problem: Indeed estimates 50-60% of job postings are ghost jobs. That's millions of fake listings wasting job seekers' time every single day.

We built GhostJobs to solve this. Our AI analyzes job postings and gives each one a "Ghost Score."

## Tweet 3/12
How it works:
1. Copy a job posting URL from Indeed, LinkedIn, etc.
2. Paste it into GhostJobs
3. Get an instant Ghost Score (0-100%)

High score = likely ghost job. Simple.

## Tweet 4/12
Each score comes with red flags so you know WHY we flagged it:
- Posted 30+ days ago
- Vague company description
- Salary not disclosed
- No specific requirements
