# GhostJobs

80% of jobs never get posted. We find them all. Every single one.

## What It Does

GhostJobs accesses the hidden job market - unlisted positions that companies never advertise. Connect with insiders and skip the ATS line entirely.

## Features

- **Hidden Market** - Access unlisted positions companies don't advertise. Your competition doesn't know they exist.
- **Direct Referrals** - Connect with insiders who can refer you directly. Skip the ATS line.
- **Real-Time Alerts** - Instant notifications when new ghost jobs match your skills. Be first to apply.
